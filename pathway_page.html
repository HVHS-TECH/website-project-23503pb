Education

Interested in learning the art of being a barista? Our education page provides all the information you need to know about our training program. Our program is designed to give students hands-on experience in coffee-making and customer service.

To qualify as a barista, students are required to complete a certain number of volunteer hours at our coffee shop. These hours provide valuable practical experience and help students develop their skills. The exact number of volunteer hours required may vary depending on the level of proficiency desired.

Join our training program and unlock the secrets of being a skilled barista. Gain the knowledge and experience needed to excel in the world of coffee-making.
